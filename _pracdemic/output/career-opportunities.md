## Career Track 1: Academic — Professor of Practice / Clinical Professor

### Why This Track Fits
A professor of practice or clinical professor appointment at a business school IS/IT management or business analytics department leverages every dimension of the profile simultaneously:

- **AACSB SA Status**: The 2024 PhD provides Scholarly Academic classification through approximately 2029 — the most constrained and valuable faculty category for accreditation compliance
- **Institutional Platform**: Faculty status creates the credibility, infrastructure, and network needed to attract consulting clients, advisory board invitations, and speaking engagements
- **Income Stability**: A 9–10 month contract with benefits provides reliable base income with summers free for consulting and research
- **3–5x Pay Increase**: Transitioning from adjunct ($3K–$6K/course) to full-time professor of practice ($120K–$175K) for equivalent teaching loads

### Target Roles

**Professor of Practice** (most common title): Teaches 3–4 courses per academic year, engages with industry partners, develops case studies, mentors students, and participates in curriculum development. No research publication requirement at most institutions; industry engagement is the primary credential maintenance mechanism.

**Clinical Professor**: Similar to Professor of Practice but more common at medical, nursing, and some law schools. Increasingly used in business schools (especially at NYU, Columbia, Georgetown) with a formal promotion track: Clinical Assistant → Clinical Associate → Clinical Professor.

**Teaching Professor / Assistant Professor of Professional Practice**: Titles vary by institution; functionally equivalent. Some carry research expectations; others are purely teaching-focused.

## Career Track 2: Consulting and Fractional C-Suite

### The Consulting Niche
The intersection of Oracle Hospitality OPERA Cloud expertise, 25+ years of global IT operations leadership, and doctoral-level research credentials creates a defensible consulting niche. The global hotel PMS market is approximately **$3 billion annually with 7–8% CAGR**. Major chains (Hyatt 1,000+ properties, Accor, Rotana, PPHE) are actively migrating to cloud-based systems, creating sustained demand for advisory services.

### Engagement Model A: Fractional CIO/CDO
- **Target**: Mid-market hotel groups (50–500 properties) undergoing cloud migration or digital transformation
- **Structure**: 1–2 days/week, 6–12 month retainer
- **Rate**: $10K–$20K/month ($120K–$240K annualized per client)
- **Differentiator**: PhD-level strategic rigor + deep OPERA platform knowledge + proven P&L management

### Engagement Model B: OPERA Cloud Migration Advisory
- **Target**: Individual hotel properties or small chains migrating from legacy PMS to OPERA Cloud
- **Structure**: Project-based, 3–6 months
- **Rate**: $200–$400/hour, or fixed-fee assessments ($15K–$50K)

- **Target**: Hotel management companies, hospitality associations (HFTP, HTNG, AHLA), university executive education programs

## Career Track 3: Industry Executive Roles

For candidates considering a full transition away from Oracle (or leveraged as negotiating alternatives), the industry executive market for this profile is strong.

### Chief Digital Officer / Chief Information Officer
- **Base**: $250K–$400K
- **Total Comp**: $400K–$800K+
- **Target Sectors**: Mid-to-large hospitality chains, healthcare IT, fintech
- **PhD Edge**: Research methodology, publication credibility, analytical rigor signals strategic thinking above the operator level
- **Note**: Deloitte research shows CDO ownership produces **88% success rate** on digital programs vs. 59% for CIO-led — driving intense demand for CDOs who bridge technology and business strategy

### Chief AI Officer (CAIO)
- **Average Base**: ~$250K–$350K
- **Average Total**: ~$354K; top-firm packages $500K–$650K+
- **Qualification Edge**: ~**40% of CAIOs hold PhDs**; IBM 2025 study shows CAIO appointments doubled from 11% to 26% of organizations in two years
- **Relevant background**: FAU AI Bootcamp (2020), Big Data Analytics Graduate Certificate (2019), Oracle neural network coaching, IoT/ESP32 projects

### VP Digital Transformation
- **Base**: $170K–$250K
- **Total Comp**: $220K–$350K
- **Target**: Hospital systems, hotel chains, cruise lines, fintech companies in South Florida
- **Note**: Role title and scope vary widely; often a stepping stone to CDO

### Industry Analyst (Gartner / Forrester)
- **Base**: $172K–$248K (Senior Director Analyst)
- **Total Comp**: $200K–$280K
- **Fit**: Leverages research rigor and industry credibility simultaneously; lower ceiling than executive or consulting but strong work-life balance and significant influence
